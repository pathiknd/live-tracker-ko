What is this page doing?

For any tickers you add, its data is pulled from Google Finance every 5 seconds and updated in the ticker box - deplay is longer to avoid getting blocked by Google for violation of service usage.

The color of the box indicates whether the ticker is up or down. The color will change based on the change in prices.

Pause and Remove buttons will pause updates or remove ticker from tracking respectively.
